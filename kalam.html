Dr. A.P.J.ABDUL KALAM

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an eminent Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Widely known as the "Missile Man of India" and the "People's President," he dedicated his life to advancing India’s space and defense capabilities.
